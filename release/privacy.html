Privacy Policy for Stay Focus

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection

"Stay Focus" does not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell any personal data. The extension operates entirely locally on your device.

2. Local Storage

We use Chrome's local storage API (chrome.storage) to save your visual preferences, such as spotlight size, color, opacity, and shape. This data remains strictly on your local machine and is never synced to external servers.

3. Permissions Usage

The requested permissions (including storage, activeTab, scripting, and host permissions) are used exclusively to render the spotlight overlay on the webpages you visit. We do not track your browsing history, monitor your behavior, or read the content of the pages.

4. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
